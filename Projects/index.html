How to cook rice

  1. Wash the Rice with a strainer
  2. Mince Garlic to put into the rice
  3. Sear the garlic in the rice cooker with some oil
  4. Add water (a little more than amount of rice)
  5. Add rice, set rice cooker to cook, and wait
  6. Enjoy!
